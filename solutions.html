Our Solutions

We provide customisable tools to minimise lead time in between workflows, enabling not only timely and cost-effective project delivery, but also quality and safety assurance.

Quality & Safety

Instant notification enables fast fix of defects. Responsible parties are notified once defects are captured during inspection.

Workflow

Keep inspections moving through to completion by personalizing workflow. Once you have completed your part, the next responsible team will automatically take it from there.

Paperless

Digitalising your company standard forms saves you from tedious data-entry work. Append photos, drawings, dates, signature and other project data and reproduce them into PDFs by a single click.

Mobility

Work anytime, anywhere on offline mode, and then synchronise everything when back online. Location-based data capture provides great convenience for on-site inspection.

Customisation

Every project is unique, so is our solution. Customise your forms, notifications and subscription to reports of your choice. The project solution is also your personal assistant.

Insights & Reporting

Data in forms and drawings can collated, analysed, reported and even integrated to your back-end system using our RESTful API. Process tracking does not require any manual effort from now on.
